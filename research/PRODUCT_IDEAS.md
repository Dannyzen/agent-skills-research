# 🚀 Product Concepts & Pitches

## 1. The Living Charter (For Advocacy/Non-Profits)
**Concept:** A "Sovereign Memory" system that combats institutional amnesia by indexing an organization's history, bylaws, and voice.
**The Hook:** "Don't hire a substitute teacher (generic AI); build a Living Charter."
**The Stack:**
- **Brain:** Private LLM (Ollama/Gemini Flash)
- **Soul:** Qdrant Vector Store (Bylaws, Board Minutes, Winning Grants)
- **Interface:** Simple Chat UI (Streamlit/OpenWebUI)

**Target Audience:**
- Schools / Education Advocacy
- Non-Profits with high turnover
- Founders transitioning out (Legacy preservation)

**Value Proposition:**
- **Continuity:** Answers "What would [Founder] do?"
- **Consistency:** Grants written in the organization's specific voice.
- **Sovereignty:** Data stays local/private; not trained into public models.

## 2. The Grant Scribe
**Concept:** Specialized RAG workflow for development directors.
**Function:** Ingests past successful grants to generate new proposals with high stylistic fidelity.

## 3. The Legacy Node
**Concept:** Cloning the wisdom of a departing leader.
**Input:** Exit interviews, email archives, manifestos.
**Output:** A Q&A bot for future leadership to query strategic intent.
